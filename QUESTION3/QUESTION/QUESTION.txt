QUESTION:Define accessibility in the context of web development.
why it is essential to create accessible websites and how it benefits
different user groups.

Answer:Accessibility in web development means designing and coding
websites in such a way that people with disabilities can perceive,
understand, navigate and interact with digital content effectively.
It make web content usable for everyone.

Importance of accessibility:
1) User experience : It helps to improve the user experience for 
everyone, including clear nevigation and content structure.
2) Business growth : To reach wider audience,increasing engagement,
loyalty and potential customer base.
3) Legal compliance : Many countries have laws requiring websites
to be accessible, avoiding legal issue and promoting equal access
4) Inclusivity : Accessible website ensure that everyone, including 
people with disabilities, can access and use online information and 
services.


Benefits of Accessible websites:
1) Mobile Users : Accessible design improves mobile experience 
through simplified and responsive layouts.
2) Motar disabilities : Keyboard navigation and easy to click elements
assist users with motor limitation.
3) Visual Impairments : Accessibility provides screen reader support 
and text alternatives, aiding blind and visually impaired users.
4) Hearing Impairments : caption and transcripts benefits those with
hearing Impairments by making audio content understandable.
5) Elderly users : Accessible websites accommodate age-related 
limitation ensuring usability for elderly users.
